Daringbadi

The Kashmir of Odisha

Quick Highlights

  • Hill station in Odisha with cool climate
  • Known for lush green valleys, waterfalls, and coffee plantations
  • Home to tribal culture and diverse flora and fauna
  • Perfect for nature lovers and adventure seekers

About Daringbadi

Daringbadi, located in Kandhamal district, is the only hill station of Odisha, often called the ‘Kashmir of Odisha’ due to its pleasant weather and occasional winter snowfall. Surrounded by dense forests, pine trees, and coffee gardens, it is an ideal destination for nature lovers and adventure seekers.

Things to Do

  • Visit the beautiful Midubanda and Badangia waterfalls
  • Explore coffee plantations and tribal villages
  • Enjoy nature trails and wildlife spotting in nearby forests
  • Experience winter snowfall (on rare occasions)

How to Reach

  • By Air: Nearest airport is Bhubaneswar (approx. 250 km)
  • By Train: Nearest railway station is Berhampur (approx. 120 km)
  • By Road: Well-connected via road from Berhampur and Bhubaneswar

Best Time to Visit

October to March – Ideal for exploring natural beauty and cool climate.

Travel Tips

  • Carry warm clothes, especially in winter
  • Book accommodations in advance due to limited options
  • Respect the local tribal culture and environment
