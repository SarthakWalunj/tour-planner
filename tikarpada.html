Tikarpada

Nature's Paradise on the Mahanadi

Quick Highlights

  • Located on the banks of the Mahanadi River
  • Famous for Satkosia Gorge and Gharial sanctuary
  • Ideal for boating, birdwatching, and jungle safaris
  • Offers eco-cottages and scenic river views

About Tikarpada

Tikarpada is a stunning eco-tourism destination located in Angul district, Odisha. Nestled along the majestic Mahanadi River and the Satkosia Gorge, it is renowned for its breathtaking landscape and the Tikarpada Gharial Sanctuary. A perfect spot for nature lovers, it offers rich biodiversity, forest trails, and riverside serenity.

Things to Do

  • Boat ride along the Mahanadi River in the Satkosia Gorge
  • Spot crocodiles and gharials at the Tikarpada Wildlife Sanctuary
  • Enjoy birdwatching and scenic nature trails
  • Relax in forest eco-cottages or go for a jungle safari

How to Reach

  • By Air: Nearest airport is Bhubaneswar (approx. 170 km)
  • By Train: Nearest station is Angul (approx. 60 km)
  • By Road: Well-connected via road from Angul, Cuttack, and Bhubaneswar
