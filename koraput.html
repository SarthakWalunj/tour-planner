Koraput

The Land of Hills and Valleys

Quick Highlights

  • Scenic landscapes with lush green valleys and waterfalls
  • Home to the famous Jagannath Temple of Koraput
  • Rich tribal culture with vibrant weekly markets
  • Ideal for trekking, nature lovers, and spiritual seekers

About Koraput

Koraput, nestled in the Eastern Ghats of Odisha, is known for its breathtaking landscapes, tribal heritage, and serene environment. With picturesque valleys, dense forests, and charming waterfalls, it is a paradise for adventure seekers and nature enthusiasts.

Things to Do

  • Visit the famous Sabara Srikhetra Jagannath Temple
  • Explore the Duduma Waterfalls and Deomali Hills
  • Experience tribal culture and vibrant local markets
  • Enjoy scenic trekking trails and camping spots

How to Reach

  • By Air: Nearest airport is Visakhapatnam (approx. 200 km)
  • By Train: Koraput railway station is well connected
  • By Road: Well-connected by road with Jeypore, Rayagada, and Bhubaneswar
