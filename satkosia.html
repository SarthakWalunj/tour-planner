Satkosia

iger Reserve of Odisha

Quick Highlights

  • Unique river gorge ecosystem in Mahanadi River valley
  • Home to Satkosia Tiger Reserve and endangered crocodiles
  • Popular for eco-tourism, wildlife safaris, and boat rides
  • Perfect for nature lovers and adventure seekers

About Satkosia

Located across Angul and Nayagarh districts, Satkosia Gorge is formed by the Mahanadi River cutting through the Eastern Ghats. It features lush forests, deep valleys, and a rich variety of flora and fauna, making it one of Odisha’s best destinations for eco-tourism and adventure.

Things to Do

  • Boat safari on the Mahanadi River through the gorge
  • Wildlife spotting at Satkosia Tiger Reserve
  • Birdwatching and crocodile sightings near the riverbanks
  • Camping and nature walks in forest eco-retreats

How to Reach

  • By Air: By Air: Nearest airport is Bhubaneswar (approx. 150 km)
  • By Train: By Train: Nearest station is Angul (approx. 60 km)
  • By Road: By Road: Good road connectivity from Bhubaneswar, Cuttack, and Angul

Best Time to Visit

October to March – ideal for safaris, boat rides, and pleasant weather.

Travel Tips

  • Book forest stay or safari permits in advance
  • Carry insect repellent and wear trekking-friendly clothes
  • Maintain silence and respect wildlife while exploring
