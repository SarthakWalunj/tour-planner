Chandragiri

Mini Tibet of Odisha

Quick Highlights

  • Famous for Tibetan monasteries and serene landscapes
  • Padmasambhava Mahavihara – one of Eastern India’s biggest monasteries
  • Home to Tibetan refugee communities and authentic Tibetan cuisine
  • Close to Mahendragiri Hills for trekking and mythology lovers

About Chandragiri

Chandragiri is located in the Gajapati district of Odisha and is known for its peaceful atmosphere, rich Tibetan culture, and beautiful natural surroundings. It’s an offbeat gem where visitors can enjoy a cultural and spiritual retreat surrounded by forested hills and traditional Tibetan settlements.

Things to Do

  • Visit the magnificent Padmasambhava Monastery
  • Try Tibetan food like momos, thukpa, and butter tea
  • Shop for Tibetan handicrafts and handmade carpets
  • Explore nearby Mahendragiri Hills for nature and mythology

How to Reach

  • By Air: Nearest airport is Bhubaneswar (approx. 265 km)
  • By Train: Nearest station is Brahmapur (approx. 80 km)
  • By Road: Regular buses and taxis available from Brahmapur

Best Time to Visit

October to March is the ideal time when the weather is cool and cultural festivals are celebrated.

Travel Tips

  • Respect local traditions and monastery rules
  • Try local food but ensure it's from hygienic places
  • Carry warm clothes as it can get chilly, especially at night
